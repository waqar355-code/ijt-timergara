Introduction

Islami Jamiat-e-Talaba established on December 23, 1947, is the largest and most influential student organization in Pakistan. It was founded by a group of 25 students gathered in Lahore with the vision of creating an Islamic society based on the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah.
